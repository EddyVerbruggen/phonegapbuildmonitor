About

Phonegapps has been developed by X-Services. We are a Dutch company specialized in the implementation of multi-platform mobile apps using PhoneGapTM and PhoneGap BuildTM.

During development of these apps we ran into the limited mobile support of PhoneGap BuildTM. That's why we built Phonegapps. Using Phonegapps you can easily trigger a new build of your app and install it on your device. The app also remembers multiple logins! Phonegapps uses the PhoneGap BuildTM API.
